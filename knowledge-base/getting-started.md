# Getting Started with CloudFlow

CloudFlow is a workflow automation platform for teams.

## Creating an Account

1. Visit https://cloudflow.io/signup
2. Verify your email
3. Complete the onboarding wizard (takes ~5 minutes)

## Free Plan Includes

- Up to 3 active workflows
- 1,000 API requests per day
- 1 team member
- Community support (forum only, no SLA)

## Pro Plan ($29/month)

- Unlimited workflows
- 50,000 API requests per day
- Up to 10 team members
- Email support with 24-hour response SLA

## First Workflow

Use the visual builder to connect triggers (webhook, schedule, email) to actions (HTTP request, Slack message, database write). Templates are available in the Template Gallery.

## Support Channels

- **Free**: Community forum at community.cloudflow.io
- **Pro**: Email support@cloudflow.io
- **Business/Enterprise**: Dedicated account manager and priority support line
