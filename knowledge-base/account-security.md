# Account Security

## Password Reset

To reset your password:

1. Go to https://app.cloudflow.io/forgot-password
2. Enter your registered email address
3. Click the link in the email within 24 hours (links expire after 24 hours)
4. Choose a password with at least 12 characters, including one uppercase letter, one number, and one special character

If you do not receive the email within 5 minutes, check your spam folder or contact support.

## Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

CloudFlow supports TOTP-based 2FA via authenticator apps (Google Authenticator, Authy, 1Password).

To enable 2FA:
1. Settings → Security → Two-Factor Authentication
2. Scan the QR code with your authenticator app
3. Save your backup codes in a secure location

## SSO

Single Sign-On (SSO) via SAML 2.0 and OIDC is available on **Business** and **Enterprise** plans only. SSO is not available on the Free or Pro plans.

## Session Management

Sessions expire after 30 days of inactivity. You can view and revoke active sessions under Settings → Security → Active Sessions.
