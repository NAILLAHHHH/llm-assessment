# Refund Policy

CloudFlow offers refunds under the following conditions:

## Eligibility

- **Annual plans**: Full refund within 14 days of purchase if you have not exceeded 10% of your usage quota.
- **Monthly plans**: Refunds are not available for monthly subscriptions. You may cancel anytime and retain access until the end of the billing period.
- **Enterprise contracts**: Refund terms are defined in your signed agreement.

## How to Request a Refund

1. Email support@cloudflow.io with your account email and invoice number.
2. Include a brief reason for the refund request.
3. Our billing team responds within 2 business days.

## Processing Time

Approved refunds are processed to the original payment method within 5–10 business days.

## Non-Refundable Items

- Add-on API overage charges
- Professional services or onboarding fees
- Partial months on monthly plans
